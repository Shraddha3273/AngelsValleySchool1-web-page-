Our School


We believe that Nature teaches in silence. Our 14 acres of sprawling green campus shares a special bond with every child. The green environment in the midst

of fields, lovingly watch over juvenile adventures, inspire imagination and helps students stay focused. We, at Angels Valley,believe that education lies

beyond the text book. Learning comes from self enquiry, reasearch and encouraging the natural curiosity of young minds. We believe that it is important to

ensure the overall development of each one of our students through the physical, mental and spiritual development of their senses.
